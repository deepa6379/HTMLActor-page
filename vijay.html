Vijay

Vijay, also known as Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, is a highly acclaimed Indian actor, playback singer, and dancer, primarily known for his work in Tamil cinema. He is celebrated as one of the most popular and highest-paid actors in the industry, boasting a massive fan following. His fans affectionately call him "Thalapathy," meaning commander in Tamil. Recently, Vijay has announced his entry into politics with the launch of his political party, Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam, alongside his continued acting career. Vijay began his acting journey as a child artist in films directed by his father, S. A. Chandrasekhar. His debut as a lead actor came with Naalaiya Theerpu (1992). While his early films didn't achieve significant success, he gained widespread recognition with Poove Unakkaga (1996). Over his career, he has starred in numerous commercially successful films and has received multiple awards. Vijay is also known for his philanthropic activities.
